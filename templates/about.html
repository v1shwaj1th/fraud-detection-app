About Insurance Fraud

  • Insurance fraud occurs when people deceive an insurer for financial gain.
  • Common types include false claims, inflating damages, and staged accidents.
  • It leads to higher premiums for everyone and undermines trust in the system.
  • Using AI/ML models can help detect suspicious patterns early and efficiently.
  • Stricter regulations and policyholder education are key to prevention.

Precautions to Avoid Insurance Fraud

  • Never sign blank insurance forms or documents.
  • Verify the credentials of agents or brokers before dealing with them.
  • Always review the claim documents and ensure all details are accurate.
  • Report any suspicious calls or claim offers to your insurance company.
  • Keep all records of communication and documentation for your policy and claims.
